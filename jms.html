Canadian Artist Janice Mason Steeves is represented by galleries in Canada and the U.S. She teaches painting workshops internationally and maintains an active Art Mentoring program.

Approach

This project required a complete redesign and update of the Artist's former site. Requirements included a clean, intuitive user interface, a readable, minimal typeface, color palette selection based on her current artwork, multiple image galleries, mailchimp signup form, a custom favicon, and contact and social media integration.

  •  Down

Solution

The design solution consisted of a one-page custom coded site featuring Bootstrap 3 modals customized to act as full pages of content. This creates a fast reacting SPA type user interface with no page loading. It further featured 3 image galleries with popups and carousel, and required significant photo editing and placement to accommodate varying image dimensions.

  •  Down

Tech Specs

The site is built with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 3.3.7, and Flexbox. It required UI/UX design, custom typography, color palette development, image sourcing, as well as photography consultation and editing.
