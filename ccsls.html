CCSLS

Creative Communication Speech & Language Services

CCSLS is a Toronto Speech Language Services company that provides powerful tools to maximize the language and literacy learning potential of children.

Approach

A redesign from their previous site, this project grew from the client's need for a more modern, professional website with a focus on advertising, new customer sourcing, blogging, and ecommerce. Requirements included an updatable blog, easy client access for content and image additions, ability to buy their original educational books, and the integration of contact and social media information.

  •  Down

Solution

The design solution consisted of a multi-page Wordpress approach, with a theme selected for its attractive front page blog display, and further customizations including: industry specific imagery sourcing, ecommerce links to products hosted on amazon and ingido, a contact form, as well as social media and google map integration.

  •  Down

Tech Specs

The site is built with Wordpress, HTML5, and CSS3. It required content migration from their previous site, image sourcing, color palette development, as well as client consultation and tutorial on Wordpress use.
