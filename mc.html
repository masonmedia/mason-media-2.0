Matthew Curry is a Christie's International Chestnut Park Sales Representative, bringing over 15 years of experience in the financial services industry to the real estate field. From buying to selling, Matthew guides his clients through every step of the process.

Approach

This project required a clean, minimal, responsive website for the face of Matthew's Real Estate business. It needed to present his range of services, copious text content laying out the many facets of buying and selling property, with links to financial services and market reports, mortgage and land tax calculators, available properties, and the Chestnut Park home portal.

  •  Down

Solution

As the client needed access to update content, the design solution consisted of a multi-page Wordpress site, with a clean, minimal theme. It consists of a front page image carousel, blog, minimal color palette, embedded social media feeds (facebook, twitter, instagram), industry specific imagery, links to multiple real estate resources, and contact information.

  •  Down

Tech Specs

The site is built with Wordpress, HTML5, and CSS3. It required photo sourcing, graphic design and image creation, complete content and copy writing, as well as client consultation and tutorial on Wordpress use.

*Note: This site has been changed significantly from the original version by the client and is not maintained by Mason Media.
