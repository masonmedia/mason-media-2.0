DJ Mycorrhiza

Born an 80s dreamer, The Mycorrhiza lives and breathes Atari, 90s hiphop, beer a la mode, Chuck Mangione, and ambient noise. He walks the dangerous line between minimalism and sci-fi, spending his time in an effort to improve the world through sound.

Approach

A website for Lisbon DJ Mycorrhiza, this project required an innovative design to match the client's inimitable style and personality. Requirements were bold graphics, colors, and imagery, a showcase of audio tracks, a blog updatable by the artist, and contact information. It also needed complete content creation and editing with a focus on abstract, artistic language.

Solution

The design solution consisted of a Wordpress theme customized for an original look and feel. It features a fullscreen video banner, bold, oversized typography, tracks embedded using HTML5 audio, a blog, as well as unique imagery and branding. The project also required a Facebook page design available here.
