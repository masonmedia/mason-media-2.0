Advance Therapy

Advance Therapy specializes in rehabilitation services for people with an acquired brain injury. They provide clients with customized support for activities of daily living, educational or vocational pursuits, and community reintegration activities.

Approach

This project required a complete overhaul and redesign of the firm's previous site. Requirements included a simple, intuitive user interface, a low amount of scrolling while still presenting a large amount of content, a specific typeface, and the firm's previous logo.

  •  Down

Solution

The design solution consisted of a one-page layout with animated dropdown sections that hide and reveal textual content upon click/tap. It features a side-canvas, smooth-scrolling menu, expanded color palette, updated, modernized branding, and images selected based on industry related themes.

Tech Specs

The site is built with HTML5, CSS3, Flexbox, Bootstrap, jQuery, and included UI/UX design, copy writing and editing, color palette development, and photo sourcing.
