Approach

This project served as the face of the client's PhD dissertation research project. Its purpose was to present the project outline, attract potential participants, and act as a filter for appropriate personality types. Requirements were parsing a copious amount of text and presenting it in web format, graphic design and image sourcing, logo creation, color scheme, a questionnaire link, co-sponsor link/logo inclusion, as well as contact information.

  •  Down

Solution

The design solution consisted of a single page, custom coded site featuring a bold grid layout, project specific imagery, custom icons, a featured questionnaire link, social media integration, and contact information. The project also required a Facebook page design for social media marketing.

Tech Specs

The site is built with HTML5, CSS3, and Flexbox. It also required significant copy editing, UI/UX/graphic design, color palette development, and image sourcing.
