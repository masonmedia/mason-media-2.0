Copetín

Copetin Restaurant and Bar serves chef Claudio Aprile's inventive, elevated Canadian cuisine informed by the diverse food personalities of Toronto's ethnic neighbourhoods. Copetin features a stylish dining room, relaxed bar and lively patio.

Approach

A design mockup for Copetín, this project fully revamped their existing minimal website. It aimed to organize the restaurant's content in a more easily digestible format, make their menu items cleaner and easier to navigate, develop and extend their color scheme and branding, while adding complexity and a bold, modern design fitting of chef Aprile.

  •  Down

Solution

The design solution consisted of a one-page layout with image carousel, full screen background images, menu presentation (without loading secondary pages), Open Table reservation button, social media integration, hours, and contact information. Branding and colour themes were used creatively and extended throughout the site.

  •  Down

Tech Specs

The site is built with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 3, and Flexbox. It required photo sourcing, graphic design, custom typography, and extended existing color and branding themes. While the project didn't move forward, it was a huge improvement on their existing site, and instructive in designing for the restaurant industry.
